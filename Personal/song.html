Sweet Baby Born Tonight

Written By: Lynette Larson

Music By: Mark Stephens

Audio:

Lyrics:

Hush, The little child is sleeping,
Sweet baby born tonight.
Laid in a manger safe keeping,
While a star above shines bright.

Mary and Joseph are happy,
Their child is here at last.
His birth to them a miracle,
Foretold by prophets past.

While the animals move softly
The babe sleeps undisturbed.
Angels o're the hillside are singing,
And shepherds hear their words.

Worship the child and glory to God,
For this king and Savior new.
This boy child in the manger asleep
Is born for each of you.

And the shepherds go and find him
And on their knees they pray,
Father in Heaven we thank
For this great and glorious day.

Wisemen also seek Him,
Following that star above.
Gifts they lay before Him,
Bowing to that child of love.

Christ was born that Christmas day,
A mission of love to show us the way.
Praise His name forever more,
Jesus, the Christ, Whom we adore.

Repeat Chorus
