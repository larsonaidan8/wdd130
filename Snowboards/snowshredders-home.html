This company has changed the dynamics of snowboarding. We make elite boards for everyone to enjoy. However unlike most companies we make our snowboards very different. Our snowboards are made from recycling other snowboards. We get snowboards that are old and retired or broken and we use them to create whole new snowboards that can be used all over again. These snowboards are meant to be able to be used for all kinds of things and all kinds of people. We have such a wide variety of snowboard that our company sells. We sell everything from begginer snowboards for little kids all the way up to expert level boards that can be custom designed for professionals.
