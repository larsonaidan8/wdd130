Overview

Purpose

We have the best snowboards and snowboard accessories that are out on the market today. We prioritize our customers and their happiness, they are the most important thing to this brand. We have standards that we ensure are upheld and we sell for a fairly reasonable price because we want as many people as possible to be able to enjoy our product and get the best out of it that anyone can get. Our product can be for those who are just starting out all the way to the professionals that are competing all around the world. With just a few very simple adjustments that you can do all by your self you can customize your board to your riding experience and goals.

Audience

The audience for this website is anyone that loves to snowboard and shred some snow up on the mountain tops. We have multiple different sizes and designs for all people and boards to match their personalities. Depending on the size and the design that you are looking for we even customize boards to fit you and your riding style best.

Content

Home page

This company has changed the dynamics of snowboarding. We make elite boards for everyone to enjoy. However unlike most companies we make our snowboards very different. Our snowboards are made from recycling other snowboards. We get snowboards that are old and retired or broken and we use them to create whole new snowboards that can be used all over again. These snowboards are meant to be able to be used for all kinds of things and all kinds of people. We have such a wide variety of snowboard that our company sells. We sell everything from begginer snowboards for little kids all the way up to expert level boards that can be custom designed for professionals.

Images for the Home page

Models (page2)

We have many different models that are available for sale. We have all kinds of different sizes and shapes depending on what you want to do with you snowboard. Different shaped snowboards serve different purposes and allow you to more easily focus on something and be able to do what you want more efficiently. We have more affordable optinos for those who don't want to make a huge investment. Then we also have more expensive versions that are rated better for tricks and more intense snowboarding, they also provide better quality and longer life span. We make all of our snowboards to the best quality that we can make it for the price that we are selling them for along with the needs of each individual board.
